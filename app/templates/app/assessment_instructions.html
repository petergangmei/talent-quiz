Instructions

This survey will help you identify your spiritual gifts. Please read these instructions carefully before beginning.

Response Scale

For each statement, select the response that best describes you:

5 Highly characteristic of me / definitely true for me
4 Most of the time this would describe me / be true for me
3 Frequently characteristic of me / true for me — about 50% of the time
2 Occasionally characteristic of me / true for me — about 25% of the time
1 Not at all characteristic of me / definitely untrue for me

Important Guidelines

  • This is not a test, so there are no wrong answers.
  • The survey consists of 80 statements that you'll rate on a scale of 1 to 5.
  • Answer quickly with your first impression — your initial response is often the most accurate.
  • Please answer every statement based on who you are, not who you would like to be.
  • Work at your own pace, but try not to overthink your responses.
  • For each statement, you'll see reflection prompts to help guide your thinking.

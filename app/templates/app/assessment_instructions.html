Spiritual Gifts Assessment Instructions

This is not a test, so there are no wrong answers. Please respond honestly to each statement.

Directions

The Spiritual Gifts Survey consists of 80 statements. Some items reflect concrete actions, other items are descriptive traits, and still others are statements of belief.

  • Select the one response you feel best characterizes yourself for each statement.
  • Do not spend too much time on any one item. Usually your immediate response is best.
  • You can skip a question if you are truly unsure, but try to answer as many as possible.
Your Response Choices
Rating What It Means
5 Highly characteristic of me/definitely true for me
4 Most of the time this would describe me/be true for me
3 Frequently characteristic of me/true for me – about 50% of the time
2 Occasionally characteristic of me/true for me – about 25% of the time
1 Not at all characteristic of me/definitely untrue for me

After completing the assessment, you'll receive a profile of your spiritual gifts based on your responses. This will help you understand your primary areas of giftedness and how you might serve most effectively.
